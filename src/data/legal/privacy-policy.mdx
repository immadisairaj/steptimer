### **Step Timer – Privacy Policy**  

**Effective Date:** 11 Mar 2026

At **Step Timer**, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.  

## 1. Information We Collect  
- **Step Timer does not collect or store personal user information** such as name or email.  
- **Payment processing** is handled by RevenueCat and/or the respective app store.  
- While Step Timer does not access or store payment card details, **encrypted financial information may be processed and stored by RevenueCat** for transaction validation and subscription management.  
- The App operates offline, and all app-related data remains on your device.  
- **No advertisements are displayed within the App.**

## 2. How We Use Your Information  
- Step Timer does not use personal data because none is collected.  
- Encrypted payment-related data is used solely by **RevenueCat** to manage purchases and subscriptions.  

## 3. Data Storage and Security  
- All app-related data is stored locally on your device.  
- We do not store user data on external servers or cloud services.  
- Any financial information handled by RevenueCat is **encrypted and secured according to industry standards**.  

## 4. Sharing of Data  
- We **do not** sell, share, or transmit user data to third parties.  
- Payment-related data is handled exclusively by RevenueCat and the applicable app store for billing purposes.  

## 5. External Links  
- The App may contain links to third-party websites.  
- We are not responsible for the content, security, or functionality of third-party websites or browsers.  

## 6. Your Rights  
- Since no personal data is stored by Step Timer, there are no user data deletion requests required.  
- If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.  

## 7. Children's Privacy  
- Step Timer is not directed at children under 13, but it may be used with parental guidance.  
- We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.  

## 8. Changes to This Policy  
- This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time, and users are encouraged to review it periodically to stay informed.
